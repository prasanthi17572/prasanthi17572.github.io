ALL ABOUT RYTHMIC THUNDERS...

This club was started in 2020 year by 2020 passed out batch students The idea behind establishing the club is to promote dance.It was formed to bring out the dancing talents and give a platform to those who have passion and love for dance.It not only focuses on entertaining people but also works on bringing out motivational message that will be useful for the student.The Dance Club welcomes new talented students every year with open heartsto bring out the hidden talents and also provide them a stage where they can showcase their dancing talents and interest. We have conducted too many events.We celebrate national and international dance daysevery year.
